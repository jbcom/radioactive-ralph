## What's happening in this tape?

1. **`ralph status`**: Shows the overall health of the orchestrator, current cycle count, active agents, and recent personality-driven events from Ralph.
2. **`ralph discover`**: Scans your configured org paths to find missing documentation, tests, or code standard violations.
3. **`ralph pr list`**: Shows all open pull requests across your portfolio, automatically classified by their CI and review status.
4. **`ralph install-skill`**: Demonstrates the versatility of Ralph's family of [ten distinct variants](index.html#ralph-variants).
